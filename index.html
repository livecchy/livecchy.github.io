串串互娱（成都）科技有限公司

公司简介

串串互娱（成都）科技有限公司是一家致力于将科技娱乐与美食文化相结合的创新型休闲游戏公司。我们以中国丰富的餐饮文化，特别是四川特色的串串文化为背景，打造创意十足、趣味横生的游戏体验，旨在为全球玩家带来前所未有的美食模拟与互动娱乐体验。

公司核心业务涵盖休闲游戏的开发与运营，注重游戏内外的沉浸式体验与文化表达。团队由一群充满热情的游戏开发者、艺术设计师和市场专家组成，秉承“娱乐与美食双重享受”的理念，在轻松愉悦的玩法中展现地道的中华文化与艺术风貌。

游戏工作室方向

  • 美术风格：主打国风、中国四川地域文化特色
  • 游戏类型：休闲类游戏，融合美食、音乐、对战等元素
  • 产品定位：融合地方文化与趣味玩法，提供治愈、轻松、社交为核心的娱乐体验

通过不断创新与突破，串串互娱将持续探索游戏与文化、娱乐与生活之间的无缝融合，打造更具品质的游戏产品，推动休闲游戏行业发展，为玩家带来更多欢乐与惊喜。

Company Introduction

ChuanChuan Interactive Entertainment (Chengdu) Technology Co., Ltd. is an innovative casual game studio dedicated to combining interactive entertainment with culinary culture. Inspired by the rich traditions of Chinese food, especially Sichuan-style skewers, we are committed to creating unique and enjoyable game experiences that bring food simulation and cultural storytelling to life.

Our core business includes the development and operation of casual games. We focus on delivering immersive gameplay and cultural depth, driven by a passionate team of developers, artists, and marketers who believe in "double delight of entertainment and cuisine."

Studio Focus

  • Art Style: Chinese traditional aesthetics, rooted in Sichuan culture
  • Game Type: Casual games blending food, music, and light competitive elements
  • Product Positioning: Culturally rich, relaxing, socially engaging casual games

With continuous innovation and cultural creativity, ChuanChuan Interactive strives to push the boundaries of casual gaming by delivering high-quality, culturally inspired content that brings joy and wonder to players around the world.
