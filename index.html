串串互娱（成都）科技有限公司

ChuanChuan Interactive Entertainment (Chengdu) Technology Co., Ltd.

串串互娱（成都）科技有限公司是一家致力于将科技娱乐与美食文化相结合的创新型休闲游戏公司。我们以丰富的中国餐饮文化，特别是四川特色的串串文化为背景，打造独具创意和趣味性的游戏体验，旨在为全球玩家带来前所未有的美食模拟和互动娱乐体验。

公司核心业务涵盖休闲游戏的开发与运营，注重游戏内外的沉浸式体验与文化呈现。我们的团队由一群充满热情与创造力的游戏开发者、艺术设计师和市场专家组成，秉承“娱乐与美食双重享受”的理念，力求在游戏中呈现地道的美食文化与轻松愉快的游戏玩法。

通过不断创新与突破，串串互娱将继续探索游戏与文化、娱乐与生活的无缝连接，未来将以更丰富的内容和更高品质的作品，推动休闲游戏行业的发展，为玩家带来更多欢乐和惊喜。

ChuanChuan Interactive Entertainment (Chengdu) Technology Co., Ltd.

ChuanChuan Interactive Entertainment (Chengdu) Technology Co., Ltd. is an innovative casual game company dedicated to combining technological entertainment with culinary culture. With a focus on the rich food culture of China, particularly the Sichuan-style skewers, the company aims to provide players worldwide with a unique, engaging experience that blends food simulation with interactive entertainment.

Our core business includes the development and operation of casual games, where we emphasize immersive experiences and cultural representation both in-game and in-game mechanics. Our team consists of passionate and creative game developers, artists, and marketing experts, who are committed to bringing the delightful experience of Sichuan cuisine into the gaming world with easy-to-play yet captivating gameplay.

Through constant innovation and breakthroughs, ChuanChuan Interactive will continue to explore the seamless connection between gaming, culture, entertainment, and everyday life, driving the casual game industry forward with high-quality content and exciting new releases, bringing more fun and surprises to players worldwide.
