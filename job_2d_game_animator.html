Job Description

ChuanChuan Interactive Entertainment (Chengdu) Technology Co., Ltd. is seeking a creative and passionate 2D Animator to join our team in crafting casual games inspired by Sichuan’s skewer culture! We’re looking for someone who can create immersive animations with Chinese traditional aesthetics and Sichuan’s vibrant cultural flair.

As a 2D Animator, you will collaborate closely with our art and design teams to produce dynamic visuals that enhance gameplay with cultural depth and playful charm.

Responsibilities

  • Design and create 2D animations for characters, environments, and effects, reflecting Sichuan cultural elements (e.g., skewers, chili peppers, street food vibes).
  • Collaborate with the art team to ensure animations align with the Chinese traditional style and maintain visual consistency.
  • Contribute to creative planning, adding dynamic flair to gameplay and storytelling.
  • Optimize animation assets for smooth performance across various devices.

Requirements

  • Proficient in 2D animation tools (e.g., Spine, After Effects, Animate, or similar software).
  • Familiar with Chinese traditional art styles and an understanding of Sichuan’s regional culture.
  • Strong aesthetic sense and creative storytelling, with the ability to integrate food and cultural themes into animations.
  • 2+ years of experience in 2D animation or game development preferred.
  • Passionate about casual games, with strong teamwork and communication skills.
